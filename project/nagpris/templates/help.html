To be able to use this app, you must create and account buy registring with a user name, valid email and password. This can be done under the register tap

Once you create an account. You can login using your personal username and password. This can be done under the login tab at the menu

Under the sell tap on the menu which appears after a successful login, you can post items to be displayed for sales and buyers will contact you by the email you provided during registration.

Search key word using the provided search bar the the menu. for example: macbook

Contact seller buy clicking the "message seller button" found at the particular product you are interested in purchasing

Remember to logout buy clicking on your first name at the top right corner of the screen and clicking "Logout.
